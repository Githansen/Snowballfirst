Growing your own food is important

Food production is responsible for 25% of the worlds greenhouse gas emission. This is due to the whole process, from production to transportation, and in the end the disposal of waste. In order to meet UNs sustainable development goals, we need to cut emissions. And since food production is behind such a large portion of it, it's possible to cut a lot from there. A good way to do this, is by transitioning to produce some food on your own. On this website, you can read more about how you can get started.
